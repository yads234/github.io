Macli-ing Dulag

An Indigenous Leader; An Environmental Defender

Here's a time line of Mac Liing Dulag's Achievments and Good Deeds:

  • 1930 - Born in Tinglayan, Mountain Province
  • 1974 - Dulag receives the prestigious Ramon Magsaysay Award for Community Leadership for his effort in defending indigenous lands and rights.
  • 1975 - He leads the opposition againts the World Bank - funded Chico River Dam Project, which threatens the ancestral lands of Kalinga and Bontok people.
  • 1980 - Despite facing threats and intimidation, Dulag continues to advocate for indigenous rights and environmental protection.
  • April 24, 1980 - Macli-ing Dulag is tragically killed by Philippine military forces, sparking outrage and protests both nationally and internationally.
  • AWARDS Honored at the Bantayog ng mga Bayani Wall of Remembrance.

"Dulag's courageous activism and sacrifice inspire generations of indigenous rights defenders and environmental activists in the Philippines and around the world."
